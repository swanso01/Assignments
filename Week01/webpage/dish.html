How to Make a Lobster Roll

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces Lobster Meat (cooked, torn into bite-size pieces)
  • 3 tablespoons Mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Butter (room temperature)
  • 2 Hot Dog Rolls
  • 1/2 head Butter Lettuce (thinly sliced)

Directions

  1. Combine lobster meat and mayonnaise in a medium bowl. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  2. Butter the outside surfaces of the hot dog rolls. Heat a medium skillet over medium-high heat. Place the rolls, 1 buttered side down, in the skillet; cook until browned slightly. Flip and cook the other side until browned.
  3. Open the rolls and fill with the lobster mixture. Add the thinly sliced butter lettuce on top.
